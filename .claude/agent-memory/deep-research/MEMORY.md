## Effective Search Strategies
- `site:domain.com` queries work well for scoping to a specific site
- For finding blog articles, search for specific technical terms rather than version numbers
- WebFetch may be permission-denied in this project; rely on WebSearch for content extraction
- Combine multiple parallel searches to cover different angles efficiently
